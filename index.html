About Us

Who we are

Barel Tech is an online learning platform empowering individuals with practical computer skills. Our hands-on courses bridge the gap between education and industry, helping learners build confidence and succeed in today’s tech-driven world.

At Barel Tech, we offer practical training in computer technologies, including Microsoft tools, Windows installation, and IT troubleshooting. Our expert-led courses focus on quality, hands-on learning, and real-world application for every learner.
